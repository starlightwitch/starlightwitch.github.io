PROTOTYPE is a digital zine created by DurmPAC to serve as an archival companion to Pop Box Gallery's exhibit of the same name. The PROTOTYPE exhibit featured artworks by forty-one Triangle area artists who each transformed a cardboard pentaprism into a unique work of art to demonstrate the important role of art of innovation.

The zine highlights seven of those pentaprisms, proudly displayed with high quality images captured by Tyra Dixon and live animations programmed by Jett Pavlica.

Mobile devices are supported, however we recommend using a modern browser on a desktop or laptop for the best experience.
